Sometimes the fastest place to shape an idea is Notion, not the repository. This post models that handoff by surfacing a Notion badge on the card and post page, while still letting the Markdown file become the source of truth for publishing.

The blog layout is designed so a future metadata sync step can enrich the page with a Notion-derived cover image when one exists.
